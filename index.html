Harmony School of Innovation - Euless is a K - 5th public charter school providing a high-quality education option for students in the surrounding areas. Harmony Public Schools is a system of 58 Texas public charter schools that provides rigorous, high-quality education focused on Our Mission Harmony prepares each student for higher education by providing a safe, caring and collaborative atmosphere.
